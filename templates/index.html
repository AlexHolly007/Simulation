Welcome to the Simulation! This is a choose your own adventure game, but you can make up
any situation you want! To try it out, start by creating a scenario in the User Input box.
Use thrid person for the best results. Then hit the “Generate” to illustrate your response.

Some examples:
‘A small creature is walking through a moist dark forest.’
‘A person is running along the top of the skyscrapers in a shiny city at sunset.’
‘The dragon roams through the bright red caves.’

After receiving a response, you will be able to enter another response in the
User Prompt area once again to continue the story how you like.
This is the User Input box. If it is the first message of the
simulation, start of with something along the lines of the examples below.

‘A small creature is walking through a moist dark forest.’
‘I am running along the top of the skyscrapers in a shiny city at sunset.’
‘The dragon roams through the bright red caves.’

After the generative AI has responded with a picture and text illustrating
your creative simulation, you can respond in this text box to continue the
simulation, for example explain a next step you would like to see happen. This
will create a new image and response, and there is no ability to go back to
the previous image and response
